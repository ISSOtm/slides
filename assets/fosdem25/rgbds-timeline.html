Oct 1996
Carsten Sørensen releases xAsm, xLink, and RGBFix for MS‑DOS.
Jul 1997
Sørensen packages them together as ASMotor.
Aug 1999
Justin Lloyd adapts it to just the Game Boy CPU as RGBDS.
Jun 2009
Vegard Nossum ports it to Linux as rgbds‑linux.
Jan 2010
Anthony Bentley's fork of it becomes the canonical RGBDS.
Sep 2010
Sørensen's GitHub project becomes the canonical ASMotor.
Jan 2015
stag019 begins RGBGFX, a GB graphics converter.
Sep 2016
RGBGFX is added to the RGBDS toolchain.
Feb 2017
RGBDS moves to the Rednex organization.
Jan 2018
RGBDS is relicensed as MIT.
Sep 2020
RGBDS moves to the GBDev organization.
May 2022
GBDev publishes rgbds.gbdev.io for docs.
